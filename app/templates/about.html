About me

I am a 17-year-old developer from Prague, Czech Republic. My specialization is creating fast, responsive, and reliable websites. I have several years of experience in making professional, minimalistic websites that are easy to maintain and free from unnecessary dependencies.

My approach to web design is very practical. I avoid frontend frameworks when possible, prioritizing simplicity, full control over my projects, and long-term reliability.

In addition to web development, I am proficient in Python for a wide range of different applications. I have experience with machine learning (AI), data visualization, desktop app development, data processing/analysis, web scraping, automation and many more.
I usually write backend for my web applications in Python using Flask/Django.

Since I often deploy and host projects myself, I am quite knowledgeable about linux. I can manage linux servers on a professional level.
